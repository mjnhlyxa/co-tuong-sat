How to Play

Board Setup

9x10 board with River dividing the two sides. Each side has 16 pieces.

Pieces

CT
Commander (Sâm) — Moves 1 square any direction, must stay in palace
S
Advisor (Sĩ) — Moves diagonally 1 square, must stay in palace
X
Minister (Xe) — Moves any distance horizontally or vertically
M
Horse (Mã) — Moves in L-shape (2+1), can be blocked
P
Cannon (Pháo) — Moves like Xe, must jump to capture
T
Soldier (Tốt) — Moves forward only; after crossing river, can also move diagonally

Special Rules

Palace: Sâm and Advisors must stay within their palace (center 3x3 area).

River: Soldiers cannot move sideways until they cross the river (center horizontal band).

Sâm Sacrifice: When your Sâm is in checkmate with no legal moves, you may sacrifice your Sâm to capture the opponent's Sâm — winning immediately!

Win Conditions

• Capture opponent's Sâm
• Opponent has no legal moves while in check
• Opponent resigns
